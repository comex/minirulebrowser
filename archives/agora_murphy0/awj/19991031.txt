Summary of Adopted Proposals
----------------------------

Proposal 3932 "Discretise Circulation Tax Onset" by harvel
  VTs spent on Voting, and P-Notes spent on Priority increases, are subject
  to the Circulation Tax in effect at the beginning of the Proposal's Voting
  Period.

Proposal 3933 "Allow Distributive Fixes" by harvel
  The weekly Proposal Batch need not be distributed all at once.

Proposal 3934 "New Blots and Infractions" by Wes
  Rule 1812 (Notices of Infraction) allows actions other than Orders to
    accompany a Notice of Infraction.
  Rule 1813 (Persistent Indebtedness) is repealed.

Proposal 3935 "Office Normalization redux" by Murphy
  The Treasuror, Payroll Clerk, and Priest are brought in line with the
    wording made standard by "Office Normalization".
  Rule 1921 ("Assessor's Commission") is repealed.  This concept was
re-added
    by "Office Normalization" shortly after it was repealed by "A Separation
    of Money, but Not of Powers".
