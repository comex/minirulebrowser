Listservers

Since Agora is a Play-By-Email game, everything we do has to go by email. Because there is so much Agora-related traffic, we have three main listservers to use, and one backup. The "Public Forum", which all Players are required to receive, consists of agora-official, agora-business, and yoyo and tue (the backup servers). Subscription to agora-discussion is optional but recommended.

As Archivist, I have more-or-less complete logs of all the servers dating back to the game's inception. The most recent week or two is kept available through these pages; older than that, you'll have to email me.
