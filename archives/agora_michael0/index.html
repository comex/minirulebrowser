The Agora nomic game

To join Agora, you need to register. This can be done by posting to the Public Forum, and requesting that you be registered. The Public Forum includes the mailing lists nomic@yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au, and agora-business@agoranomic.org. You can subscribe to the latter by visiting this site, or by mailing agora-business-request@agoranomic.org.

Official documents

These documents are not guaranteed to be up-to-date, or even correct, but I am consciously trying to copy across fresh versions into mail as they arrive (and no, I don't want to try and automate it!).

Assistant Directory of Personnel's Report
The ADoP keeps track of Office holders and runs elections for posts that fall vacant.
Grand Warden of the Oligarchy's report
Report of the Officer responsible for tracking the Oligarchy.
Herald's report
The Herald keeps track of Kudos, now Agora's only currency, used to pay fees for performing a variety of in-game actions.
The Notary's Report
Report detailing all of the sub-groupings within the game.
The Proposal Pool
The current set of undistributed proposals.
The Registrar's Report
A list of all the current players, and other bits and pieces of official information.
Ruleset
The rules man, all that is noble and holy in the game. The full ruleset is annotated with roughly 100KB of additional information. Voting reports since I became Rulekeepor are available here.
Scores
We have re-introduced the notion of points and scores to Agora, but for teams of Players rather than individuals. This is the most recent report of such available.
Voting Potentials report
Players can achieve the right to vote on certain classes of proposal. This report records their current voting rights.
