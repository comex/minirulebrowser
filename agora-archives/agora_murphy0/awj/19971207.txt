     THE AGORA WEEKLY JOURNAL
     VOLUME 42, ISSUE 10
     Sunday edition
     December 7, 1997


PROPOSALS
---------

12/02  Steve re-Proposes "Left In A Huff!" and "Electioneering and the
         Public Voting Experiment", previously submitted as Proposals
         3609 and 3610 (which had no chance to pass because Transfer
         Orders were broken).

12/03  General Chaos protoes "Simplify Judicial Salaries":  Judicial
         Salaries are paid within one week of their being earned (not
         at the beginning of the Nomic Week), and vacated whenever a
         decision is overturned upon Appeal.

       General Chaos protoes "Probate, Take Two", a Probate reform (as
         if you couldn't guess).

       Agora will vote FOR InterNomic Proposal 352 (to add Bluesmobile
         Nomic to the list of voting members).

12/04  Blob re-protoes "Perpetual Offices", allowing the Registrar to
         declare Without Objection that a given Office is held in
         perpetuity (its Term of Service never expires) until it
         changes hands for some other reason.
 
       Proposal 3624 [G. Chaos] No Passing the Buck
                                  (Recordkeepors may reject Transfer Orders for
                                   ambiguous amounts; if such a TO is accepted,
                                   the Recordkeepor's determination of its
                                   amount is definitive)
       Proposal 3625 [G. Chaos] Synonymity
                                  (Define "Active" and "Inactive")
       Proposal 3626 [G. Chaos] Award Oerjan the Title of Exorcist
       Proposal 3627 [G. Chaos] Pragmatization of Transfer Orders
                                  (Recordkeepor's determination of success or
                                   failure of a transfer is definitive)
       Proposal 3628 [Murphy]   Fix Nominations
                                  (Eligibility of Nominees measured at time of
                                   Nomination)
       Proposal 3629 [G. Chaos] Persistent Judges
                                  (Judges remain assigned to a CFJ till CotC
                                   recuses em)
       Proposal 3630 [Crito]    Ratifications
                                  (of Herald's Report and P-Note Report of
                                   December 1)
       Proposal 3631 [Steve]    Left In A Huff!
                                  (same as Proposal 3609)
       Proposal 3632 [Steve]    Electioneering and the Public Voting
                                  Experiment
                                  (same as Proposal 3610)
 
       Chuck protoes "Public Presence", extending the Public Voting
         Experiment (Rule 1741) to cover Declaration of Presence.

12/05  Ackanomic submits InterNomic Proposal 353 (to amend Rule 215 by
         replacing "Rishonnomic" with "Rishonomic").
 
       Steve Proposes "Permanent Public Voting", which would remove the
         delayed-self-repeal clause from Rule 1741.
