     THE AGORAN DAILY JOURNAL
     Volume 42, Issue 2
     Wednesday, October 8, 1997

Recent events:

September 30
  * Vlad's Grace Period ends.

October 1
  * Crito and Morendil ceased to be Boors (Rule 1682).
  * Morendil Nominates for Speaker-Elect and Ambassador.
  * General Chaos Nominates for Speaker-Elect.

October 4
  * Kolja A. goes On Hold.
  * Elde is deregistered for Lawlessness.  The effect of this on Morendil's
    status as Elde's Executor and Executor in Probate, and Elde's status as
    Blob's Executor and Executor in Probate, is disputed.

October 6
  * Crito alleges that Blob has Abandoned the game.
  * Harlequin re-Nominates for Cartographor.

October 8
  * Morendil becomes Ambassador.
  * Harlequin remains Cartographor.
  * Due to lack of candidates, a new Chancellor Election begins.
  * General Chaos re-Nominates for Chancellor.
  * Voting for Speaker-Elect begins, with General Chaos and
    Morendil as Candidates.
  * CFJ 933's Judgement of TRUE is sustained by the Appeals Court.
    Proposal 3533 (Pragmatic Currencies) destroyed all Voting Tokens.
    Proposals 3534-3554 received no Votes of any sort.
    Seven Wins by Proposal (Rule 1663) have occurred - three for
      Steve, three for Andre, and one for Morendil.
  * Chancellor General Chaos mints 500 Voting Tokens.
