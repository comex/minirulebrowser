OFFICES
-------

         those OFfices begin.

         elJefe and Justiciar General Chaos may not Nominate.

INTERNOMIC
----------

InterNomic defaults as judge for SFJ 10 ("Non-members of InterNomic may submit
statements for judgement.").  The InterNomic rules don't say what happens now.

SFJ 11 (concerning InterNomic's recognition of "The Church of Odo on the Worm
Hole" - which may no longer be recognized by its parent, Ackanomic, and whose
nomic-hood has not yet been verified) is assigned to FDC Nomic (a small,
non-voting member run by Murphy).
