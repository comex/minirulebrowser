PROPOSALS
---------

11/17  Agora will vote FOR InterNomic Proposal 351.

       Proposal 3601 [Chuck]    Read the Ruleset Week
                                  (First full week of February)
       Proposal 3602 [Chuck]    The Stars and Stripes Forever
                                  (13-VT bonus to creator of Rule 1776)
       Proposal 3603 [G. Chaos] Innocent Error Defense
                                  (No Crime if Player reasonably believed
                                   innocence)
       Proposal 3604 [G. Chaos] Allow Mandated Vacation
                                  (Re-execution of required, executed-but-then-
                                   vacated Payment Order is not required if
                                   the Rules required the Payment Order to be
                                   vacated)
       Proposal 3605 [G. Chaos] No Doubling-Up
                                  (Transfer Order can't pay multiple Payment
                                   Orders)
       Proposal 3606 [G. Chaos] Clarify PI Infraction
                                  (Currency Recordkeepors can report Persistent
                                   Indebtedness only for the Currency of which
                                   they are Recordkeepor)
       Proposal 3607 [G. Chaos] Fix Groups
                                  (Allow SLCs other than Ordinances to be
                                   associated with Groups)
       Proposal 3608 [G. Chaos] Fix Vizier Specification
                                  (Split Rule 721 into two logical parts)
       Proposal 3609 [Steve]    Left In A Huff!
                                  (Patent Title to Waggie, Gecko, Kelly, Swann,
                                   KoJen, Zefram and Vlad)
       Proposal 3610 [Steve]    Electioneering and the Public Voting Experiment
                                  (Assessor may reveal Votes that were sent to
                                   the Public Forum anyway)
       Proposal 3611 [Andre]    Probate and Registration
                                  (Non-Players can't be Executors in Probate;
                                   Players can't be in Probate)
       Proposal 3612 [Swann]    The Repeal-O-Matic II
                                  (Automatically generates semi-random repeal
                                   Proposals)
       Proposal 3613 [Swann]    Power of Attorney
                                  (Controlled form of making someone else one's
                                   temporary Executor)

       Crito Proposes "Persistent Style":
         * Style is not reset quarterly.
         * Bonus only for exceeding one's previous highest Style.

       Swann Proposes "Objects", a sweeping Currency reform.

       General Chaos protoes "Synonymity", explicitly making "Active" and
         "Inactive" synonyms for "On Hold" and "Off Hold", respectively.

       General Chaos protoes "No Passing the Buck":
         * Recordkeepors may refuse to record Transfer Orders or Payment Orders
             not specified as a fixed numeral.
         * If they accept such Orders, then their determination of the amount
             is the amount, no matter what.

       General Chaos protoes "Lowered Quorum" (to 1/3 of Active Players or 1/5
         of all Players).

11/19  General Chaos protoes awarding Oerjan the title (not Patent Title) of
         Exorcist.

11/20  Proposal 3597 [Andre]    Blot Amnesty Extension fails quorum
                                  (All Fugitive Blots are destroyed)
       Proposal 3598 [Morendil] Finish Amnesty fails quorum
                                  (All Fugitive Blots are destroyed; Herald
                                   vacates all due Rule 1473-mandated Payment
                                   Orders for Indulgences)
       Proposal 3599 [elJefe]   Clarify Probate for Groups adopted
                                  (Prevent Group Wills from inadvertently
                                   replacing Ordinances)
       Proposal 3600 [Swann]    More Interesting Proposal Mechanisms fails
                                  quorum
                                  (Non-Proposing Entities may submit Proposals
                                   via their Executor)

       General Chaos protoes an updated "No Passing the Buck".

       General Chaos protoes "Pragmatization of Transfer Orders":  A Currency
         Recordkeepor's determination of the success or failure of a Transfer
         Order is definitive, even if based on wrong information.

11/21  Steve Proposes "Resignation and consent":  If a resigning Officer
         appoints a successor, the successor assumes Office only if e consents.

       Michael Proposes "Resignation of Offices cleared up":  Same general
         effect as Steve's "Resignation and consent"; shorter, but the
         successor must give consent prior to being appointed.

       elJefe Proposes "No nonplayer responsibilities":  Non-Players are
         barred from any role or position to which a Rule assigns duties or
         powers.

11/22  General Chaos protoes "Probate, Take Two", a Probate reform.

11/23  General Chaos re-protoes "Probate, Take Two".

       General Chaos protoes "Persistent Judges":  Judges are not removed from
         a CFJ due to delinquency till two Players demand it in the PF.  (Other
         penalties for delinquent Judgement still apply.)

       General Chaos protoes "Simplify Judicial Salaries", requiring them to be
         reported within a week (ASAP imposes an ordering requirement).
