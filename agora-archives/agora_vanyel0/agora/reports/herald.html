Herald's Reports

The Herald's job is to keep track of Kudos. Kudos are a measure of Players' honour, as perceived by other Players. A Player may transfer Kudos from any one player to any other player except emself. Two such transfers may be made per week. Players start the game with 20 Kudos. For more info about the Herald, check out Rule Category "Honour", more specifically Rules 1062, 1378, 1377, 1008, and 1379.

The Banker's Report contains all Kudo transfers occurring since the previous Report, and a listing of current Kudo holdings.
