Scorekeepor's Reports

The Scorekeepor's job is to keep track of Points. Until recently, this often involved player-to-player transfer of Points, but as of the Marks Reform of late August 1995, Points are now nontransferable except to and from the Bank as mandated by the rules. For more info about the Scorekeepor, check out the Category "The Scorekeepor and Points", and specifically Rules 901, 1475, 1511, and 726.

The Scorekeepor's Report contains all Point changes occurring since the previous Report, and a listing of current Point holdings.
