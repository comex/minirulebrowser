Registrar's Reports

The Registrar's job is to keep track of Players, and assorted information about them. A Player Registers by sending a message to the Public Forum stating such, and from then until that Player deregisters, the Registrar keeps track of eir nickname, eir email address, any Patent Titles or Offices e holds, and what Groups or Contests e belongs to. For more info about the registrar, check out the Rule Category "The Registrar", and specifically Rules 559, 676, 1502, and 1494.

The Registrar's Report contains all Player info which the Registrar is required to maintain.
