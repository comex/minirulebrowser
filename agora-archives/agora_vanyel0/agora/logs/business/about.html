Agora-business

In December of 1994, it was noted that a lot of important stuff was still being sent to nomic-discussion instead of nomic-official, because they weren't technically official documents (such as Officer reports). This meant that an Agoran really had to be subscribed to both, nulling the advantage of the separation in the first place. Rather than send this non-official stuff to nomic-official, a new list was created, called nomic-business, for important stuff that wasn't really official. All players are required to be subscribed to nomic-business.

As of the Big Move, the business list is now known as "agora-business". To subscribe, send an email message to majordomo@gecko.serc.rmit.edu.au (a mouthful! But you'll be bookmarking _that_ domain. :) with the text

subscribe agora-business

as the body.
