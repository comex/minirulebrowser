Tue

In the beginning, there was Yoyo, and it was good. But lo, after many months there came to be a surfeit of traffic on Yoyo, and other difficulties. Thus were created the primary lists nomic-official and nomic-discussion, and later their brother nomic-business. Yoyo was left to languish, a poor backup server millions of cubits from its more active cousins. Then suddenly, in the summer of the fourth year, there came to pass a great crisis, for the teleport lists were smote down for the neglect of their owner Coren. And the Players flocked back to Yoyo in great numbers, once again trafficking mightily through the transpacific fiber. From the darkness, there arose a cry: "But now there is no backup server, to catch us should the benevolent Yoyo fall!" And then was heard a single voice: "I shall provide." Player Andre spake unto his Service Provider, de Technische Universiteit Eindhoven; and thus was created Tue. Tue was to serve as backup even unto the venerable Yoyo. Yea, the very Public Forum extendeth its arm to enclose the new-fledged Tue.

That thou mayest subscribe thyself to Tue, send Email unto majordomo@win.tue.nl that saith

subscribe agora

as the body of the missive.
