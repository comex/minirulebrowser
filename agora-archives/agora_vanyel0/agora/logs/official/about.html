Agora-official

In August of 1994, it was decided that to not be swamped with Nomic mail, it would be nice to receive only the official documents. Thus, nomic-official was created for the transmission of Officer's Reports and Proposal distributions and results; later this task passed to its successor, agora-official. All Players are required to be subscribed to agora-official.

To subscribe to agora-official, send an email message to majordomo@gecko.serc.rmit.edu.au with the text

subscribe agora-official

as the body.
