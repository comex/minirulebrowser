Steve's Piggy Bank Contest

  1. The contestmaster is Steve. In case Steve is no longer a Player, the contestmaster shall be the Speaker of Agora Nomic, if willing.

  2. The entry fee is 1 point.

  3. Only Steve may be a contestant.

  4. The Contest Fund makes transfers of Points or Currencies, and accepts transfers of Points and/or Currencies to it, as directed by the sole discretion of the Contestmaster, provided that the Contestmaster is Steve. Otherwise the Contest Fund may not make transfers.

  5. In case the Contest is dissolved, the Contest Fund is divided equally among all Contestants.

  6. The Contest is dissolved when the Contestmaster posts a message so announcing to the Public Forum.

  7. The Regulations may be changed at any time by Steve, and in no other way.
