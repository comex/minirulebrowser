Win Bookie Regulations

  1. The Entry Fee for this Contest shall be 5 Marks.

  2. At any time after joining the Contest, each Contestant may send the Contestmaster a Bet Application, marked as such and naming a Player who is the Target for that Bet Application. For each Bet Application beyond the first that a given Contestant sends to the Contestmaster, that Contestant must transfer an additional 5 Marks to the Contest Treasury.

  3. Exactly one week after a Bet Application is sent to the Contestmaster, it becomes a Bet. The Target of a Bet is the same as the Target of the corresponding Bet Application, and the Owner of a Bet is the Contestant who sent the Bet Application.

  4. When a Player Wins a Game of Agora Nomic, the following shall occur:

    • If any Contestant is the Owner of a Bet whose Target is the winning Player, half the treasury is divided among the Contestants, weighted linearly according to the number of Bets each Contestant Owns with the winning Player as Target.

    • Every Contestant must transfer 1 Mark to the Contest Treasury for each Bet of eirs that is still in effect.

  5. A Contestant may leave the Contest at any time by sending a message to the Contestmaster notifying em. When a Contestant does this, all Bet Applications sent by that Contestant, and all Bets Owned by that Contestant, cease to exist.

  6. The Contestmaster may transfer the Contestmastership to any consenting Player of Agora Nomic.

  7. The Contestmaster shall send a list of all current Bets and Bet Applications to any Contestant upon request.

  8. The regulations may be changed at any time by unanimous agreement of the Contestants, and the consent of the Contestmaster.
