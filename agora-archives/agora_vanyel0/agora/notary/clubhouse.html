The Clubhouse Ordinances

  1. The Vizier of this Group shall be Zefram.

  2. These Ordinances may be changed at the Vizier's will.

  3. Non-members may join the Group, with the Vizier's consent.

  4. Currencies in the Group's Treasury may be used at the Vizier's will.

  5. The Group's Coins may be created or destroyed at the Vizier's will.

  6. The Vizier may expel members from the Group at any time.
