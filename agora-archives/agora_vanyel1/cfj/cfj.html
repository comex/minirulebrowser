CFJs

The judicial system of Agora is fairly complex. In order to get a statement judged, one must first submit a Call For Judgement (CFJ) to the Clerk of the Courts. It is then assigned to a Judge, who returns a reasonable Judgement on the Statement. If there is still a dispute, the decision can be Appealed; then three people pass further Judgement on the matter.

This is a semi-complete archive of CFJs that have taken place. Now that the doctrine of stare decisis is in place, Andre has composed a summary listing of most of the CFJs that have happened so far.

Until I come up with a better way to do it, I'm just going to give a link to the FTP-style listing of my CFJ directory. Have fun.
