Rulesets

The Rules were last updated on May 5, 2001
and include effects of Proposals through Proposal 4146, and Proposal 4149.

If you wish to see all of Agora's current Rules on a single page, there are three different options for the Ruleset's format.

The Logical Rulesets list the Rules in something approaching in logical order--ideally, they are presented in an order which will make it easy for someone new to Agora to read through and understand. Because of the interlinked nature of the Rules, however, this is only possible to a limited extent.

The Full Logical Ruleset gives a brief explanation of the way each Rule is presented, and a table of contents of the Categories. For each Category, a brief description is given. For each Rule, its Number, Power, and Title are given; its text; official and unofficial annotations; and its history, so far as it is known. (~350K)

The Short Logical Ruleset gives only the Number, Power, Title, text, and official annotations for each Rule. (~270K)

The Numerical Ruleset lists the Rules in numerical order. The format of the Rules is the same as in the Full Logical Ruleset. (~340K)

You can also look at Agora's Initial Ruleset, which was in effect at Agora's founding on June 30, 1993. (Only 12K, if you can believe that.)
