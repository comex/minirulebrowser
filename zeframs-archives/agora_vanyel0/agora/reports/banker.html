The Agora Banker

The Banker's job is to keep track of Marks. Marks may be transferred from any Player (or anything else) who has Marks to anyone (or anything) else. For more info on the Banker, see the Rule Category "Marks and the Banker", more specifically Rules 1548, 1473, 1510, 1080, 1034, and 1470.

The Banker's Report contains all Mark changes occurring since the previous Report, and a listing of current Marks holdings.
