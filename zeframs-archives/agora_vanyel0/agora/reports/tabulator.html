Tabulator's Reports

The Tabulator's job is to keep track of Blots and Win Tokens. When a Player commits some Crime, e is usually penalized with Blots. These stay on eir record until paid off (with Points); a Player with Blots may never Win. Win Tokens are sort of the opposite--if a Player gets enough Win Tokens, e Wins. Win Tokens can be created and transferred to, or destroyed and transferred from, any other Player. To find out more about the Tabulator, check out Rule Categories "Winning a Game, and the End of the Game", and "Blots and the Immaculate"; specifically, Rules 1435, 1436, 1439, 1440, 1441, 1437, 1491, 1492, and 1493.

The Tabulator's Report contains all Blot gains and Win Token transfers since the previous Report, and a listing of current Blot and Win Token holdings.
