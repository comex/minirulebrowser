The Agora Notary

The Notary's job is to keep track of Organizations. An Organization is an Entity which has coercive and/or binding force over some subset of Agoran Players. For more info on the Notary, go to the Notary Web Page.

The Notary's Report contains a listing of all Public Organizations (currently the Groups and Contests), and their Jurisdictions.
