Agora-discussion

After the traffic became too much for poor yoyo in August 1994, nomic-discussion became the listserver where Agorans argued interpretations, evaluated proposal ideas, and discussed Nomic philosophy. In the Big Move it became agora-discussion. It is not a requirement of Players to be subscribed to agora-discussion, so no official business occurs there; it is, however, highly recommended that all Players subscribe.

To subscribe to agora-discussion, send an email message to majordomo@gecko.serc.rmit.edu.au with the text

subscribe agora-discussion

as the body.
