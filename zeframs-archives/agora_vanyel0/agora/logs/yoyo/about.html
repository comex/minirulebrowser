Yoyo

For the first year of its existence, Agora conducted all business, discussion, etc. over the Yoyo server. By August of 1994 there was clearly a lot of mail going through, so we got the other mailing lists going at teleport.com, with one for discussion and the other for official stuff. After various other developments, a business list got added, and eventually a fifth list to serve as backup. Now Yoyo and Tue are the backup servers, for when the others go down. All Players are required to be subscribed to Yoyo.

To subscribe to yoyo, send an email message to majordomo@yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au with the text

subscribe nomic

as the body.
