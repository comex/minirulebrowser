Agora Nomic

Agora is one specific instance on the net of Peter Suber's Nomic. Agora Nomic has been continuously played since June 1993. There have been thousands of Proposals, and there are hundreds of Rules... membership seems to hover around twenty Players or so, and more are always welcome!

The Archives

As Archivist, my job was to, well, keep archives. And here they are! Unfortunately, I haven't had the time to really keep up with Agora lately (like, since mid-1997); I've dropped out of the game (and thus am not Archivist). However, I do continue to keep logs of the mailing lists, because I'm still interested in the game and hope to eventually rejoin it.

As a result, most of this is out of date. I'm not going to remove it, since sometimes out-of-date stuff is better than no stuff at all, but be aware that except for the mailing lists you can probably find more up-to-date info elsewhere. If you have any questions, you can mail me at blahedo@brown.edu.

Note: Some things may have gotten jumbled in my move to Brown U.; if so, please send me a quick email and I'll fix it asap.

Items of interest... (Some of these are off-site links, so I cannot guarantee that they will always be up.)
  • The Listserver Logs contain recent discussion and other transmissions related to Agora.
  • The partial archive of past CFJs may provide some insight into how the game is played.
  • Various Theses sponsored by Agorans.
  • The current Ruleset is maintained by Rulekeepor Chuck Carroll, and is the quintessential Agora Nomic document, telling everything you can or can't do.
  • The Agora Guidebook is good to read first, to get you acquainted with some of the more peculiar aspects of Agoranism. :) (Currently offline, under revision)
  • The Officers' Reports contain the current state of things in Agora.
  • The Proposal archive holds a bunch of old proposals.
  • The Bio Page has email addresses, homepages, and lots of intra-Agoran and extra-Agoran biographical info, about Players, Watchers, and a few ex-Players.
