Chuck's First Savings and Loan Contest Regulations

  1. The Contestmaster is Chuck. The Contestmaster may not change.

  2. Only Chuck may become a Contestant. The entry fee is 1 Mark.

  3. The Contest Regulations may be changed at will by Chuck. If Chuck changes his Agora Nickname, all instances of "Chuck" in these Regulations, except those referring to the Contest itself, shall automatically be changed to his new Nickname. The Contest Regulations may not be changed except as described in this Regulation.

  4. Currencies shall be spent from the Contest Fund as Chuck sees fit. Such transfers shall be detected and reported by Chuck.

  5. This Contest shall be dissolved when Chuck decides that it shall be dissolved.

  6. The terms "Contest," "Contest Regulations," "Regulations," and "Contest Fund" in these Regulations refer specifically to Chuck's First Savings and Loan Contest and its Regulations and Fund.
